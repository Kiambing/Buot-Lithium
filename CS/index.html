PSHS-CRC School Year 2022-2023

The things that happened throughout my ninth-grade year are given here.

STEM CAREER FIELD TRIP

On September 23, 2022, there was a STEM Career Field Trip, which took place at Caraga State University.
Through interactive field trip experiences that offer chances to explore and engage in critical thinking, problem-solving,
and collaboration across a range of scientific concepts.

DORMERS ACQUAINTANCE NIGHT

On September 27, 2022, Dormers Acquaintance Night was conducted, it was held in the gymnasium This was an extremely entertaining night for all of the dormers.
We got to know one other and even danced till our feet couldn't handle it any longer. This was a great night with my friends, and I'm glad we were able to conserve a little voice for the next day's lessons.

STEM FEST 2022

STEM Fest 2022 was hosted on campus from September 28, 2022 to September 30, 2022. This was a three-day festival with all of the booths run by our seniors, contests,
and simply us having fun. We had a good day by playing games and consuming dishes from several booths.
The cheesecake from the Home Economics food stand was my fave.
