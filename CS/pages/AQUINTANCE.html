Aquintance Party

On September 27, 2022, Dormers Acquaintance Night was conducted, it was held in the gymnasium. This was an extremely entertaining night for all of the dormers. We got to know one other and even danced till our feet couldn't handle it any longer. This was a great night with my friends, and I'm glad we were able to conserve a little voice for the next day's lessons.

Although the fact that this was a party just for dorm room residents was very sad, nothing was going to stop us from having a wild time. We spent the entire night having fun and taking lots of pictures.
