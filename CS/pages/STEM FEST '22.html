STEM FEST

On campus, STEM Fest 2022 was held from September 28 to September 30, 2022. This event lasted for three days and featured senior-run booths, competitions, and just us having fun. We enjoyed our day playing games and eating food from several booths. My favorite item at the Home Economics concession booth was the cheesecake.

Given that we were exhausted from partying that night, the STEM FEST was held the day after the acceptance party. The event was delightful and long-lasting.
